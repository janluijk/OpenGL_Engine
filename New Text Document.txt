Requirements for a shader object:

-- SHAPE AND APPEARANCE --
    - Vertex data
        - Indices
    - Normal vector
    - (optional) Texture
-- TRANSFORMATIONS --
    - Model, projection, view
    - Normal Vector transformation
-- MATERIAL PROPERTIES
    - Shininess
    - Texture || ambient, diffuse
-- LIGHT PROPERTIES --
    - Light positions
    - Light direction (directional light)
    - Light color
    - Player position
    - Brightness
        - Ambient
        - Diffuse
        - Specular
        - Attenuation
            - c0, c1, c2
